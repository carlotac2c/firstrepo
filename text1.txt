Economics is the study of how individuals and societies allocate scarce resources. In modern economies, markets play a central role in coordinating production and consumption decisions. However, markets do not always function perfectly. Governments often intervene to correct market failures, promote fairness, and stabilize economic fluctuations. Technological innovation has transformed global trade, communication, and finance, increasing productivity but also creating new challenges. Understanding these dynamics helps economists design policies that foster sustainable growth and long-term prosperity.
